# Privacy Policy — Noura Bin Saidan Art Studio

**Last updated:** 2026-07-07

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Noura Bin Saidan Art Studio** mobile application ("the App", Bundle ID: `com.nourabinsaidan.studio`) handles your information. The App is provided by Noura Bin Saidan Art Studio ("we", "us", or "our").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Contact form:** If you use the in-app contact form, we receive the name and message you voluntarily enter. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is transmitted via your device's native email app directly to our official email (info@nourabinsaidan.com).

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **No automatic tracking:** The App does **not** collect analytics, usage data, device identifiers, or location data automatically.
- **No third-party SDKs:** The App does not include any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

### 1.3 What We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect, store, or process:
  - Personal identifiers (Apple ID, email — unless you contact us)
  - Location data
  - Photos or media library access
  - Health data
  - Financial/payment information

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Any information received through the contact form is used **only** to:
- Respond to your inquiry about workshops, services, or collaborations.
- Process booking requests you initiate.

We do **not** use your information for marketing, profiling, or selling to third parties.

---

## 3. External Links & Third-Party Services

The App contains links to external platforms (Instagram, X, TikTok, Snapchat, YouTube, LinkedIn, the official store at nsartstore.com, WhatsApp, and Google Maps). When you tap these links, you leave the App and are subject to the **privacy policies of those third-party services**. We are not responsible for how third-party platforms handle your data.

The App does **not** embed third-party trackers. Links open in the respective apps or your web browser.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- The contact form does not store your data within the App. It launches your device's email client with a pre-filled message.
- No data is stored on our servers through this App.
- We use reasonable technical measures to protect any information you send us via email.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Request access** to any personal information we hold about you.
- **Request deletion** of your personal information.
- **Opt out** of any communication by not using the contact form.

To exercise these rights, contact us at **info@nourabinsaidan.com**.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on this page with an updated revision date.
